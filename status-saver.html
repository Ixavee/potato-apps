Save the statuses you care about, without the clutter.

Status Saver is a lightweight offline helper for Android users who want to save selected local status media to their own device without ads, accounts, analytics, tracking, paywalls, subscriptions, or data collection.

What it focuses on

A calm utility for a local, offline task.

  • Works offline for core features.
  • Shows and saves selected local status media on the user's own device.
  • Files are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Has no ads, analytics, tracking, user accounts, login, paywalls, or subscriptions.
  • Does not collect personal data from users.
  • Keeps the experience fast, readable, and intentionally simple.
