Overview

Status Saver is designed to work offline and does not collect personal data from users.

The app does not require an account, does not include ads, does not use paywalls, and does not use analytics or tracking tools.

Information We Collect

Potato Apps does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Payment information
  • Device ID
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising data

Offline Use

Status Saver works offline. The app does not need an internet connection for its main features.

Because the app works offline, no user data is sent to Potato Apps servers.

Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

Status Saver does not contain ads.

The app does not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking pixels
  • Third-party tracking SDKs
  • In-app purchases
  • Paywalls
  • Subscriptions

App Permissions

The app may request Android permissions only when needed for its main features.

For example, Status Saver may need access to selected local media or folders so it can display and save status files on your device.

This access is used only on your device.

Files are not uploaded, collected, stored, or shared by Potato Apps.

Data Sharing

Potato Apps does not share user data with any third parties because the app does not collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Status Saver does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's features change. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
